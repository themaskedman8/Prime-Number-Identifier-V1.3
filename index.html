Glossary

Natural Number: A Natural Number is a number that is an integer which is greater or equal to one.

Composite Number: A Composite Number, like its name suggests, is a Natural Number that is divisible by any other number other than 1 and itself.

Prime Number: A Prime Number is a Natural Number that is only divisble by 1 and itself and taking any root (thats greater than one) of it will never result in a rational number.
